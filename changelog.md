# 1.0 (12/3/2025)

## Lazuli Core
Uses:
- Used to make the Lazuli Staff
- When held and right-clicked: 
    - 5 XP levels will be taken
    - You'll have Resistance 1 - 4 for 30 seconds depending on the core level unless you have Resistance
        - Depletes durability by 4 multiplied by the level
            - Level 1: 4 (64 uses max / 32 minutes)
            - Level 2: 8 (32 uses max / 16 minutes)
            - Level 3: 12 (21 uses max / 10.5 minutes)
            - Level 4: 16 (16 uses max / 8 minutes)
        - Level 4 grants Regeneration level 2 for the same amount of time
        - Only when the item is held in the main hand

Durability: 256
